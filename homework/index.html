About Us

The Darkmoon Academy is a prestigious institution dedicated to the study of the arcane arts and the supernatural.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education in magic, alchemy, and the occult.

We offer a wide range of courses, from basic spellcasting to advanced potion-making.

The Darkmoon Academy was founded as a central hub of knowledge for all things concerning the supernatural.
