Welcome To Darkmoon Academy

Your Journey Into The Occult Begins Here

The Darkmoon Academy was founded by Headmaster Salvatore in order to spread the truth about what Magic is, and to educate those on common misconceptions about its history, and usages.

We are dedicated to the study of the arcane arts and the supernatural, providing a comprehensive education in magic, alchemy, and the occult.

During your time in your selected course, you will learn various skills that will make you more adept as a mage, and will guide you into a better understanding of your abilities.

We offer a wide range of courses, from basic spellcasting to advanced potion-making.

Our mission is to provide you with a safe place of acceptance throughout your journey. No one will be turned away for any kind of belief, practice, or strange hobby.
